Seyappaduporul

Seyappaduporul is Sashainfinity’s hybrid tutoring center initiative, designed to provide accessible and effective math education to students in tier 2 and tier 3 cities. This innovative approach combines in-person and online instruction to create a comprehensive learning experience.

Advantages


Hybrid Learning Model

Seyappaduporul combines the benefits of in-person and online instruction, providing students with a flexible and engaging learning experience.

Personalized Tutoring Approach

Seyappaduporul offers personalized tutoring methods, utilizing a dedicated Learning Management System (LMS) to engage students actively.

Accessible Math Education

Seyappaduporul makes math education accessible to students in smaller cities, providing them with the same high-quality educational experience as students in larger cities.

Key Features

Key Features of Seyappaduporul

In-Person Instruction


Seyappaduporul offers in-person instruction, providing students with the opportunity to interact with teachers and peers.




Online Instruction


Seyappaduporul also offers online instruction, allowing students to learn from anywhere and at any time.






Adaptive Learning Algorithms


Seyappaduporul’s adaptive learning algorithms adjust the difficulty level of lessons based on individual student performance, ensuring that each learner stays challenged and engaged.


Performance Tracking and Analytics


Seyappaduporul provides detailed performance tracking and analytics, enabling educators to monitor student progress and identify areas where students need additional support.
